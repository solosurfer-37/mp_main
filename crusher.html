How It Works

Working Principle

Powerful impact crushing combined with robust collision mechanics for consistent breakdown of tough materials.

01

High-Speed Impact

The material is fed into the crushing chamber where it is forcefully impacted by high-speed rotating hammers.

02

Collision Breakdown

The material is effectively broken down through repeated impact from the hammers and subsequent collision with reinforced breaker plates.

03

Bottom Discharge

Once the desired particle size is achieved, the crushed material easily passes through the adjustable grate bars at the bottom and is discharged.

The Hammer Crusher operates by feeding material into a heavy-duty crushing chamber, where it is instantly met with high-speed rotating hammers. This rapid motion shatters the material upon impact.

The material continues to break down through repeated collisions with the internal breaker plates. Once it reaches the required size, it seamlessly passes through the grate bars positioned at the bottom of the chamber, ensuring a uniform and consistent final product.
