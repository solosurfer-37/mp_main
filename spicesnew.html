Industry Overview

Serving the Spice
Processing Industry

The spice industry requires specialized processing equipment capable of handling a wide variety of raw materials while maintaining product quality, colour, aroma, and consistency.

At Modern Pulveriser, we manufacture grinding and mixing solutions for spice processors, masala manufacturers, seasoning producers, and food ingredient companies.

Our equipment supports applications ranging from coarse grinding to ultra-fine pulverization and homogeneous blending.

Impact Pulverisers and Air Classifying Mills are commonly used depending on the required fineness.

Can your machines be manufactured in SS304?

Yes, food-contact parts can be supplied in SS304 and SS316.

Do you provide complete spice processing solutions?

Yes, we can recommend equipment based on your capacity and product requirements.

Can the machine handle multiple spices?

Yes, with proper cleaning procedures, the same machine can process different spices.
