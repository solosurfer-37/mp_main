How It Works

Working Principle

High-efficiency screening powered by multi-plane vibration — ensuring precise classification and continuous discharge.

01

Vibratory Motion

An unbalanced motor generates multi-plane vibration, causing material fed onto the screening surface to move continuously across the mesh.

02

Material Separation

Smaller, fine particles easily pass through the required micron size screen, ensuring an accurate and consistent grade of output.

03

Multi-Stage Discharge

Oversized particles are discharged separately from the screening area. Multiple decks allow for multi-stage separation in a single operation.

The Vibro Sifter operates on a vibratory motion generated by an unbalanced motor. Material is fed onto the screening surface, where multi-plane vibration causes particles to move across the mesh.

Smaller particles pass through the screen while oversized particles are discharged separately. Multiple decks can be used for multi-stage separation in a single operation, making it ideal for thorough and uniform sizing tasks.
